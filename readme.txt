== Description ==

<p>Affiliate Blocks is a Gutenberg plugin that gives you the functionality to add conversion-optimized elements in your blog posts. Increase your CTR and improve your sales by using this plugin. Professionally designed Pros and Cons block, notice block, notification block, call to action, and buttons with gradients to have more CTR in your affiliate blog posts.</p>

<p>Simply pick pre-designed blocks from the Affiliate that speed up the process of affiliate website building with easy-to-use settings and customizations.</p>

<p>With the Affiliate Blocks, we took the hassle out of the affiliate website building process. It gives you a number of unique and creative blocks for Gutenberg that help you build and design pages or posts without a single line of code.</p>

<p><strong> This plugins blocks.</strong></p>
<ul>
    <li><strong> WY Table of Content - very Easy table of content block </strong></li>
    <li><strong> WY Button - Add professional looking buttons in your affiliate articles to get more CTR.</strong></li>
    <li><strong> WY Star Rating - Give star rating to the products you review and help your readers to buy the products.</strong></li>
    <li><strong> WY Notice Block - Notice block can be used to put any special notices inside the blog post. For any special, discount, deals and promotion. This block can be used at the end of the article to write an awesome conclusion.</strong></li>
    <li><strong>WY Notification Block - Notification block for notices in your article posts which brings the quick attention, This block can be used in your articles to make beautiful headlines as well.</strong></li>
    <li><strong>WY Single Product - This is a perfectly designed block to promote any single product it service in your blog posts. This block will capture the attention so you are going to get more CTR and ultimately more sales.</strong></li>
    <li><strong>WY Pros &amp; Cons - This well-designed pros and cons block help you add the features and drawbacks of the service/product you are reviewing.</strong></li>
    <li><strong>WY Call to Action - Add professional "Call to Action Block" in your blog posts to get more attention and clicks. Grab the special attention of users by showing them this attractive CTA box.</strong></li>
</ul> 

== Installation ==
1. Upload this plugin to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
